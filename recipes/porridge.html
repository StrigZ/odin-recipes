Brown Rice Breakfast Porridge

Use up that leftover brown rice and try something new for breakfast. Experiment with it to make this brown rice breakfast porridge your own. You can try using any mixture of dried fruit instead of the blueberries.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup 2% low-fat milk
  • 2 tablespoons dried blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 pinch cinnamon
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Â¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Combine cooked brown rice, milk, blueberries, honey, and cinnamon in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low; simmer for 20 minutes.

  2. Beat egg in a small bowl. Whisk hot rice mixture, 1 tablespoon at a time, into egg to temper until about 6 tablespoons incorporated. Stir tempered egg, butter, and vanilla into rice mixture; continue cooking over low heat to thicken, 1 to 2 minutes.
